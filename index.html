About Pakmart Wholesalers

Pakmart Wholesalers was proudly launched in 2025 with a bold vision: to become the most reliable and farmer-friendly wholesale distributor in the region. Strategically located in Bomet County, Kenya, we are perfectly positioned to serve the agricultural heartland of the Rift Valley, making high-quality farming inputs easily accessible to local farmers, agrodealers, and retailers.

We proudly stock trusted brands such as CKL Africa and are continuously expanding our product range to include more agricultural supplies, farm tools, seeds, fertilizers, and veterinary products. Whether you are a smallholder farmer or a large commercial grower, Pakmart is your one-stop shop for all farming essentials ‚Äî all at unbeatable wholesale prices.

At Pakmart, our mission is built on reliability, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We believe that farming is the backbone of our economy, and we are here to make it more productive, affordable, and rewarding. Our goal is to empower farmers with timely access to quality products, ensuring they get the best results in every planting season.

By choosing Pakmart Wholesalers, you are partnering with a distributor who understands your needs, values your business, and is committed to walking with you every step of the way. As we grow, expect to see even more product categories, better deals, and expanded delivery coverage across Bomet and beyond.
